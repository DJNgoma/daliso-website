1. Information We May Collect

Depending on the app and the features you use, I may collect limited categories of information such as:

  • Information you provide directly, including support emails, feedback, account details, or other content you submit.
  • Technical and diagnostic information such as device type, operating system, app version, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Usage information about how core features are accessed so we can maintain, secure, and improve the app experience.
  • Purchase or subscription status where needed to unlock paid features, managed through the relevant platform or payment provider.
  • Permission-based data such as camera, microphone, photo library, notifications, or location only when required for a specific feature you choose to use.

2. How We Use Information

I use information collected through these apps to operate and improve them, including to:

  • Deliver requested features and maintain app functionality.
  • Respond to support requests, bug reports, and other communications.
  • Monitor reliability, diagnose crashes, and improve performance.
  • Protect the security and integrity of our services.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms where needed.

3. Sharing and Third-Party Services

I may use third-party services to help operate these apps, such as hosting providers, analytics tools, crash reporting services, authentication providers, app stores, or payment platforms. Those providers may process information on my behalf or under their own privacy terms. I do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Retention

I keep information only for as long as needed to provide the relevant app, fulfill support or legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain business or security records. Retention periods may vary based on the type of data and the services being used.

5. Your Choices

  • You can manage app permissions in your device settings.
  • You can stop using an app or uninstall it at any time.
  • You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion where applicable.
  • You can manage subscriptions or purchases through the platform where you obtained the app.

6. Children’s Privacy

These apps are not intended to collect personal information from children without appropriate authorization. If you believe a child has provided personal information through one of these apps, contact me and I will review the request promptly.

7. International Use

These apps and their service providers may process information in more than one country. By using the app, you understand that information may be handled in jurisdictions with different data protection laws than your own.

8. Changes to This Policy

I may update this policy from time to time. When I do, I will update the date on this page. If an app-specific policy or in-app notice conflicts with this general policy, the app-specific notice will control for that app.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, or data-related inquiries, contact info@africantechno.com.
